Welcome to YourCompany.com

Our broker services enhance your logistics process by offering customized solutions and support for your current business model. Whether your shipments are large or small, you can count on us for all your transportation requirements. Our transportation services are tailored to the needs of each customer and our management team is here to provide quality customer service with a variety of options to execute their logistic needs.


In addition, our transportation management software is integrated in a manner that provides each customer with 24 hour “around-the-clock” monitoring capabilities. Customers can login using their username and password at any time to monitor the status of a shipment.

About Us

Our company was founded by William Jones in 1989. After dedicating nearly 18 years to transportation information systems as a Chief Information Officer (CIO) ,  Mr. Jones left the white collar world of logistic satellite technology to form his own company, WJ Brokers, Inc. Today, WJ Brokers, Inc. has satellite agency offices located in all major regions of the U.S. Please contact us to locate a freight agent in your region.
